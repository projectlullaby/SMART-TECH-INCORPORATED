About the Company

Founded in 1967 in Ravenwood, Smart Tech Inc. has remained at the forefront of technological innovation for decades. Established by Dr. Elias Virelli and Dr. Conrad Halberg, the company focuses on the development of advanced interactive systems.

Smart Tech Inc. has played a central role in the development of Ravenwood since its early years. Through strategic acquisitions and partnerships, the company has integrated innovative ideas from independent developers.

Product Highlight (1980s)

Lullaby, the Singing Lamb

An interactive companion capable of responding to voice input and providing musical engagement. Originally designed as a child-friendly entertainment unit, Lullaby utilized early adaptive audio recognition systems.

The system was capable of learning patterns of speech and replicating tonal responses based on user interaction. Extended exposure testing was conducted in controlled environments across multiple facilities.

Internal testing phases extended beyond initial deployment. Behavioral inconsistencies were noted during prolonged operation cycles.

Further documentation regarding these inconsistencies remains unavailable.
